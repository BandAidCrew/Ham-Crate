Administrative details

Our client is the band Ham Crate.
Our contact person is Frank Crate.
Ham Crate is a newly started band, starting to get a few streams on Soundcloud. Frank is the main vocalist in the band. He is the spokesperson for the band and will be the one we communicate with and he will be our link to the band. It is also his responsibility to make sure the demands from the band are communicated to us.

Site’s purpose and goals

Frank wants a site to promote his band Ham Crate.
The main goals of the web site is increasing the popularity of Ham Crate. This means getting more streams on Soundcloud, more views on YouTube and more people on concerts. This can be accomplished by supplying new music and pictures from concerts, as well as an appealing design and impression of professionality. Another goal is keeping existing supporters and satisfy their wishes. To accomplish this, we plan to supply existing supporters with information about the band. This includes history, information about band members, and upcoming events. A third goal is earning some money by selling products.

Typical audience

The primary audience for this site will be people who have heard a little bit about the band and are curious about the band and their music. It will therefore be important that the site accommodates the needs for new listeners and make them want to continue to listen to the band. The band has informed us that this is very important. They want a page that is clean, innovative and inviting so that new potential fans will be intrigued to looking at the site more and to learn more about it.

The secondary user which this site will be for is already established fans. They will need to find more information about the band if they want and also upcoming shows for the band.

Specific Preferences

  • The webpage should work on most mobile and desktop browsers.
  • They want their logo on the site and as a favicon.
  • They want a merch-page to showcase their merchandise. This will only be sold at their concerts.
  • The website should be easy to use. etc:
    • Find out who is the drummer.
    • Find out when the next concert is.
    • Listen to their song demo.
  • The website should use the following color palett:

      Consisting of: #DC3522, #D9CB9E, #374140, #2A2C2B, 1E1E20

Content and organization

Here are the pages which will be included in the site:

  • About
    • A short description about the band and its history. Maybe a discography.
  • Members
    • Picture and short bio about each band-member.
  • Upcoming events
    • A list of shows and location. Maybe a link to where you can order tickets.
  • Music
    • Put up songs from a site like soundcloud.
  • Photos
    • An awesome-looking gallery of pictures from shows and on the road. Maybe a display of pictures from the band’s instagram account.
  • Videos
    • Music videos and vlogs from life “on the road”
  • Merchandise
    • Here we will have a page to showcase all the merchandise that dedicated fans can buy: such as t-shirts, music, posters or an all exclusive signed ham crate with real ham from the ham producer Stabburet.
The band would not like a typical web page from the 70’s with a lot of menus, boring fonts and colors like greenday.com or jamiroquai.com. They would preferably like a page where everything is on one page and menu elements in the header links to a different part of the same page.

Server for finished site

The finished site will be located at http://hamcrate.rocks. This will be hosted and maintained by the a representative of the band which will be assigned after our project is finished. Until the site is finished we will run the site and maintain it until the project is done and the band is happy.
